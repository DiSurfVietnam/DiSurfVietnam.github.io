Explore Vietnam Water Sports

Your Next Aquatic Adventure:
Lessons and Rentals

Welcome to Đi Surf, your gateway to the ultimate ocean adventure in Nui Chua National Park, Ninh Thuan, Vietnam. Here, we blend the thrill of surfing with the serenity of nature, offering an array of experiences from catching the perfect wave to unwinding on a tranquil beach. Our surf school is dedicated to helping you learn and improve, while our water sport equipment rentals let you explore the sea at your own pace. Dive in and discover your adventure with us.

Beachside Camping and BBQ Bliss

Surf, Stay, and Sizzle: Camping and BBQ

Immerse yourself in the ultimate beach getaway at Đi Surf! As the sun dips underneath the water, extend your surf session into a relaxing camping experience under the stars and savor delicious BBQ meals. Listen to the sounds of the ocean as you discover the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation at our seaside haven. Join us to experience the joy of the waves and create unforgettable memories by the sea.

Discover the Extraordinary

Embrace, Explore, Experience:
Đi Surf Tours

Embark on a journey of discovery with Đi Surf's array of exhilarating tours in Nui Chua National Park. Dive into the vibrant underwater world with our snorkel tours, where you'll encounter marine life in crystal-clear waters. For those who enjoy land adventures, our hiking tours lead you through hidden trails and scenic vistas, offering a glimpse into the park's natural wonders. Our guided tours promise excitement, exploration, and unforgettable memories in this pristine paradise.

About Us

Our Oceanic Journey:
From Waves to Memories

At Đi Surf, we are driven by our love for the ocean and the thrill of surfing. Nestled in the pristine coastal beauty of Vinh Hy, Vietnam, our team is dedicated to sharing our expertise and enthusiasm for water sports with visitors from near and far. At Đi Surf, we're not just about the sport; we're about crafting memories that last a lifetime.
