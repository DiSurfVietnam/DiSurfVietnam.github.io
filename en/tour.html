Dive into an Underwater World

Snorkel & Paddling Boat Tours

Dive into a mesmerizing world of coral reefs and colorful marine life with Đi Surf's captivating snorkel tours in Nui Chua National Park. Swim alongside tropical fish, marvel at the intricate coral gardens, and witness the beauty of the underwater ecosystem up close. Whether you catch an epic sunrise on a boat tour or paddle out on a SUP, the views of the bay and glimpses of the underwater world will surely create memories that will stay with you long after you resurface.

Step into Majestic Mountains

Hiking Tours

Traverse hidden trails, meander through lush forests, and discover the beauty of cascading waterfalls as our expert guides lead you on a path of exploration and discovery. Breathe in the crisp mountain air, soak in panoramic views, and immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature's embrace. Whether you seek a challenging trek or a leisurely hike, join us on a guided tour and let the captivating beauty of Nui Chua National Park unfold before your feet.
