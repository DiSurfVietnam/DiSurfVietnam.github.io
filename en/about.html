Our Story: Behind the Scenes with Đi Surf

At Đi Surf, our love for adventure and the thrill of riding the waves inspired us to create a unique surfing experience in the heart of Vinh Hy, Vietnam. The name "Đi Surf" holds a special significance as 'Đi' translates to 'go' in Vietnamese, symbolizing our belief in the transformative power of movement and exploration. We invite you to join us and embark on a journey where every wave is a new story waiting to be written.

My name is Hill and I am a chef, surf instructor, adventurer, and happy dog owner. I am passionate about sharing the beauty and tastes of Vinh Hy to you whether through guiding you through the jungles, teaching you to surf, or showing you new flavors of Vietnam, I will be happy to chat with you.

Surf, Sun, and Smiles Await at Đi Surf. Your Gateway to Endless Adventures to the Mountains and the Sea!
