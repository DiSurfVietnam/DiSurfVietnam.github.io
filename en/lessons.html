Catch Your First Wave: Beginner Surf Lessons

Building the Fundamentals

In our beginner surf classes at Đi Surf, participants will start with the fundamentals of ocean awareness, wave dynamics, and proper board positioning. Under the guidance of our experienced instructors, beginners will learn essential techniques such as paddling, popping up, and riding the whitewater.

Through hands-on practice and individualized feedback, students will gradually build confidence in the water, mastering the art of catching and riding their first waves. By the end of the class, beginners will have gained a solid foundation in surfing basics, setting them on a path to further exploration and enjoyment of this exhilarating sport.
