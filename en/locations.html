Paradise within Vietnam

Nui Chua National Park

Nui Chua National Park, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve in Ninh Thuan Province, Vietnam, is a treasure trove of natural wonders waiting to be explored. Nui Chua National Park offers beaches, hiking trails, waterfalls, lakes, and coral reefs for outdoor enthusiasts to discover and enjoy.

Within this pristine environment lies the picturesque town of Vinh Hy, serving as a gateway to the park's diverse attractions. Among the park's highlights are two captivating tourist destinations: Stone Park (Công Viên Đá) and Rai Cave (Hang Rái). Whether exploring the unique rock formations of Stone Park, diving into the underwater wonders near Rai Cave, or embarking on a scenic hike through the park's diverse landscapes, come visit and be enchanted by the beauty and tranquility of this UNESCO Biosphere Reserve.


Rugged beauty meets aquatic wonders

Rai Cave (Hang Rái)


Located only 5km from Vinh Hy, Rai Cave in Nui Chua National Park beckons adventurers with a range of exciting activities that celebrate the beauty of the natural surroundings including the surrounding grape farms, rice fields, rock formations, and freshwater stream right along the coast of the ocean. Camping under the starlit sky offers a serene experience, where the soothing sound of the waves lulls you to sleep and the promise of a majestic sunrise greets you in the morning.

For surfing enthusiasts, the Surf House at Đi Surf is a hub for surfers looking to catch the perfect wave. The Surf House is equipped with a variety of surfboards, including long boards, short boards, and foam boards, catering to surfers of all skill levels. Considered one of the best surfing spots in the area, the oceans next to Rai Cave offers ideal conditions for riding the waves and experiencing the thrill of surfing in a picturesque setting.

The ocean surrounding Rai Cave invites visitors to explore the underwater wonders of the coral reefs through swimming and snorkeling. The vibrant colors and diverse marine life of the coral reefs create a mesmerizing underwater landscape. Meanwhile, paddling on a SUP or kayak in the freshwater stream next to rice fields and grape farms provides a refreshing break and a chance to connect with the peaceful surroundings of Nui Chua National Park. In the vicinity, you'll find the start of the trail to ascend to the peak of Nui Chua Mountain, a breathtaking cascading waterfall, and a large peaceful lake that our tours can guide you to.

To view the unique rock formations of the fossilized coral reef up close, the entrance fee for Rai Cave is 40k dong paid to the national park. Inside there is a restaurant serving refreshments, accomodations, and a guided walkway along the rock formations. Rai Cave is also home to rehabilitating sea turtles as this unique area is one of the few areas where sea turtles nest in Vietnam. As you explore Rai Cave, you'll encounter mesmerizing rock formations creating a dramatic backdrop for your adventures and provide a glimpse into the geological wonders of the region. It is a very popular tourist location during sunrise and sunset where many people take pictures of the unique nature.


Rocky shores with amazing vistas

Stone Park (Công Viên Đá)


Given it's name, Stone Park, the are is full of large stone formations that define this unique destination. As you explore the intricate shapes and textures of the rocks, most popularly visited during the golden hours of sunrise and sunset, the play of light and shadow accentuates the beauty of Stone Park, making it a photographer's paradise and a serene retreat for nature enthusiasts. Entrance fees to the national park cost 40k dong.

Our facilities at Stone Park cater to your comfort and convenience, with a cafe bar area, Rua Da Cafe, that serves refreshing drinks and coconuts and delicious food upon request. For those seeking a more immersive experience in the national park, camping is permitted in this area, allowing you to spend the night under the starlit sky and wake up to the tranquil surroundings of the park. Đi Surf can provide accomodations and set up for your camping needs.

Moreover, Stone Park is conveniently located within walking distance to the beach and tidal pools, offering opportunities for relaxation and exploration and is home to a rare sea turtle nesting beach. Whether you're basking in the sun on the sandy shores or discovering the fascinating marine life in the tidal pools, the natural beauty of this area is sure to captivate your senses and create lasting memories.


Charming Coastal Town

Vinh Hy


Vinh Hy is a charming coastal town located within Nui Chua National Park in Ninh Thuan Province, Vietnam. Located 30km from Phan Rang and 40km from Cam Ranh, Vinh Hy is a fishing village known for its serene ambiance, scenic beauty, and fresh seafood.

Vinh Hy offers visitors a tranquil retreat surrounded by lush greenery of the mountains and crystal-clear waters of the bay. Nestled between majestic mountains and the sparkling sea, Vinh Hy exudes a sense of peace and tranquility that captivates all who visit.

The town serves as a gateway to the park's natural attractions, providing an ideal starting point for exploring hiking trails, waterfalls, coral reefs, and more. Whether you seek adventure or simply a moment of relaxation, Vinh Hy offers a harmonious blend of outdoor exploration and natural beauty that promises an unforgettable experience. Đi Surf can help you plan your adventures whether it be camping, water sport equipment renting, dining, surf lessons, or adventure tours, let us help you plan your ideal vacation.

How to Get Here

Transportation to Vinh Hy

Plane


The nearest airport is Cam Ranh International Airport in Nha Trang, approximately a 1.5-hour drive (~60km) to Vinh Hy.

From the airport, you can arrange for a private transfer or rent a car to reach us.

Train


The closest train station is Ga Tháp Chàm in Phan Rang, an hour drive (~45km) from Vinh Hy.

You can rent a car or motorbike, or take public transportation via Futa/Phương Trang bus #3 at Phan Rang bus Station (Bên Xe Tỉnh Ninh Thuận).

Bus


Direct buses to Vinh Hy from Saigon with Thien Tri Bus or An Anh Bus.

Direct buses to Vinh Hy from Dalat with An Anh if 5+ passengers or else drop off at Phan Rang.
