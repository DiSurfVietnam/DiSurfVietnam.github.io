Choose From Multiple Places

Camping Locations

Discover the ultimate in tailored camping with Đi Surf. Our offerings range from the rustic charm of traditional camping to the luxurious indulgence of glamping. Be it the sandy shores of Hang Rái or the majestic cliffs of Stone Park. Your camping adventure is yours to craft, ensuring every moment is as unique as you are.


Beach Camping at Hang Rái

Immerse yourself in nature's beauty at our beachfront camping site, offering a breathtaking view of the starlit sky above. Wake up to the breathtaking sunrise against the backdrop of Hang Rái, as the soothing sound of waves sets the perfect rhythm for relaxation and rejuvenation. Experience tranquility and adventure as you unwind in this coastal paradise, surrounded by dramatic rock formations and clear waters.

Cliff Camping at Stone Park

Discover the mesmerizing beauty of Stone Park, perched atop a majestic cliff within Nui Chua National Park. Surrounded by rock formations and panoramic vistas of the ocean, our camping site offers a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in nature's grandeur. With easy access to the nearby beach, you can witness stunning sunrises over the blue waters, creating a magical backdrop for your camping experience.
